## FAQ

**Do I need an account?**
No. The app works immediately without any registration.

**Does it work without internet?**
Yes. Ixercise is fully offline. No network connection is ever required.

**Does it track my workouts or progress over time?**
No. Ixercise does not track history, streaks, or statistics. It helps you execute your current routine.

**Is it really free?**
Yes. No in-app purchases, no premium tier, no subscription.

**Can I see the source code?**
Yes. [github.com/ilsrbn/ixercise](https://github.com/ilsrbn/ixercise)

**Which iPhones does it support?**
Any iPhone running iOS 16 or later. Live Activities and Dynamic Island require iPhone 14 Pro or newer.

**Is there an Android version?**
Not currently. iOS only.
