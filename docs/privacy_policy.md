# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: April 22, 2026**

## Overview

Ixercise does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All data created while using the app stays on your device and is never sent to any server.

## Data Stored on Device

Ixercise stores the following data locally using your device's storage:

| Data | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| Selected exercises | Remember your exercise preferences |
| Training plans | Store workout configurations you create |
| Training schedules | Remember your workout schedule |
| Feedback settings | Save your sound and reminder preferences |
| Notification IDs | Manage scheduled workout reminders |

This data never leaves your device. Deleting the app removes all stored data.

## Notifications

Ixercise can send local notifications to remind you of scheduled workouts. These notifications are:

- Scheduled locally on your device
- Never sent through any external server
- Controlled entirely by you — you can disable them at any time in iOS Settings

## Audio

Ixercise plays local sound effects during workouts. The app does not record or access your microphone.

## Live Activities

Ixercise uses Live Activities to display workout progress on your Lock Screen and Dynamic Island. This information is generated locally and is not transmitted anywhere.

## Internet Access

Ixercise does not require an internet connection and does not communicate with any server or external service.

## Third-Party Services

Ixercise does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking services.

## Children's Privacy

Ixercise does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new "Last updated" date.
