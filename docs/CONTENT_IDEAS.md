## Production Notes

- Record at 1x speed, speed up 1.5–2x in edit for snappy feel
- Keep captions under 150 characters for mobile readability
- Use on-screen text hook in first 2 seconds to stop scroll
- Shoot simulator at iPhone 17 Pro sizing for Dynamic Island visibility
- Export at 1080×1920 (9:16) for all platforms
