## Design Notes

- Use high-contrast overlays — white text on dark tint or black text on light tint.
- Keep headline font large (≥40pt at 1x), subline smaller (~24pt at 1x).
- Required iPhone sizes for App Store: 6.9" (1320×2868), 6.7" (1290×2796), 5.5" (1242×2208).
- Current screenshots are iPhone 17 simulator at native resolution — upscale or re-shoot on required device sizes before submission.
