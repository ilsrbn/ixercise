# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: April 25, 2026**

## Overview

Ixercise is a fully offline iOS app. It does not collect, transmit, sell, share, or analyze user data. All data you create while using the app stays on your device.

---

## What data Ixercise stores

Ixercise stores the following data **locally on your device** only:

| Data | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Workout routines | Store the workouts you create |
| Exercise configurations | Remember your exercises, reps, and timers |
| Rest settings | Store rest duration preferences |
| Workout reminders | Remember scheduled reminder times |
| App settings | Save your sound and theme preferences |

This data is never sent to any server. Deleting the app removes all stored data from your device.

---

## No account required

Ixercise does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information. The app works without any registration.

---

## Notifications

Ixercise can send local notifications to remind you of scheduled workouts. These notifications are:

- Scheduled entirely on your device
- Never routed through any external server or push notification service
- Under your full control — you can disable them at any time in iOS Settings → Notifications

---

## Audio

Ixercise plays local sound effects during workouts. The app does not access, record, or transmit microphone input.

---

## Live Activities

Ixercise uses Live Activities to show workout progress on your Lock Screen and in the Dynamic Island. This information is generated locally on your device and is not transmitted anywhere.

---

## Internet access

Ixercise does not require an internet connection and does not communicate with any server, API, or external service during normal use.

---

## Third-party services

Ixercise does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, attribution, or tracking services. There are no SDKs for Firebase, PostHog, OneSignal, Sentry, Mixpanel, Amplitude, or similar services.

---

## Children's privacy

Ixercise does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

---

## External links

The app may link to GitHub or the App Store. Those services have their own privacy policies. The Ixercise app itself does not transmit personal data when you follow those links.

---

## GitHub issues and developer contact

If you contact the developer via email or open a GitHub issue, any information you provide is voluntary and handled outside the app. It is used only to respond to your message.

---

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here and in the App Store listing with a new "Last updated" date.
